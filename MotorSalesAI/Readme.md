# AI Strategy & Business Automation Portfolio

## Overview

This repository serves as a central portfolio for Artificial Intelligence and Business Automation initiatives.

Its purpose is to standardize how automation projects are identified, designed, implemented, and measured across the organization.

Each project follows a common framework while maintaining its own documentation, architecture, and implementation details.

---

## Portfolio Objectives

* Standardize AI and automation delivery practices
* Create reusable frameworks and templates
* Prioritize initiatives based on business value
* Promote knowledge sharing across teams
* Accelerate implementation through reusable assets
* Measure business impact consistently

---

## Methodology

All projects follow a common lifecycle:

1. Process Discovery
2. Opportunity Assessment
3. Prioritization
4. Implementation
5. Measurement and Continuous Improvement

## Project Template

Each project folder should include:

* Business problem
* Objectives and expected outcomes
* Current-state process
* Proposed solution
* Systems and integrations
* Automation opportunities
* KPIs and success metrics
* Roadmap and timeline
* Risks and dependencies
