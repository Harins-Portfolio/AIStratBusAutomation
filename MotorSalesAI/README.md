# Sales Cycle Automation Strategy

AI and business automation initiative for a B2B motor parts distributor.

## Overview

This project aims to redesign and automate the commercial lifecycle using Artificial Intelligence, Salesforce, and ERP integrations.

The objective is to reduce manual tasks, improve data quality, increase operational efficiency, and enhance the customer experience throughout the entire sales process.

## Objectives

* Reduce manual work across the commercial process
* Centralize customer information in Salesforce

## Commercial Lifecycle

The sales cycle is structured into five stages:

1. Commercial Outreach
2. Prospecting & Technical Validation
3. Commercial Negotiation
4. Sales Closing
5. After-Sales & Customer Retention

## Automation Scope

### Stage 1: Commercial Outreach

* Define the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
* Identify target accounts through LinkedIn Sales Navigator
* Enrich contact information using Apollo.io
* Synchronize leads with Salesforce
* Automate multichannel outreach sequences
* Prioritize opportunities using AI lead scoring

### Stage 2: Prospecting & Technical Validation

* Capture customer requirements in Salesforce
* Validate product availability through ERP integration
* Confirm technical feasibility automatically
* Generate alerts when products are unavailable
* Centralize meeting notes and technical requirements

### Stage 3: Commercial Negotiation

* Generate proposals automatically using ERP pricing data
* Ensure target margins remain above 25%
* Automate approval workflows
* Track negotiation history in Salesforce
* Generate contracts for electronic signature

### Stage 4: Sales Closing

* Create orders automatically in the ERP
* Synchronize customer and order information
* Notify operations teams in real time
* Generate invoices and supporting documentation

### Stage 5: After-Sales & Customer Retention

* Automate post-sales follow-ups
* Launch customer satisfaction surveys
* Centralize incident management
* Identify repurchase opportunities
* Schedule preventive maintenance activities

## Key Decisions

* Salesforce is the central platform for the commercial lifecycle.
* The ERP is the single source of truth for inventory, costs, and orders.
* LinkedIn Sales Navigator and Apollo.io are used for lead generation and enrichment.
* Salesforce–ERP integration is the project's highest priority.
